Scrambled Eggs Recipe

Description

A warm bowl of bland ramen. I do not want to write a full two paragraphs
describing a bland bowl of ramen. It is just Maruchan ramne with no extra ingredients.
However I will describe the picture used just so that I can fill up this space.

The bowl that holds the ramen has a brown rim with blue and white lines on
the inside of the bowl. Also on the inside of the bowl is ramen along with two chopsticks.
All right I think this drives the point.

Ingredients

  • A single pack of Maruchan ramen
  • Bowl
  • Water
  • Pot
  • Stove
  • Fork or Chopsticks

Steps

  1. Fill pot up with water
  2. Turn on the stove
  3. Place pot on stove
  4. Wait for water to boil
  5. Place ramen in boiling water
  6. Wait 2 to 3 minutes
  7. Add included "seasoning" to Ramen in pot.
  8. Stir Ramen
  9. Pour ramen into bowl.
  10. Using fork or chopstick, insert Ramen into your face cavity.
  11. Say "hmmmmmm" after swalloing.

Congrats you have now eaten Ramen + possible micro-palstics :D.
